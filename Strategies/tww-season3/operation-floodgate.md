## Overview

Operation: Floodgate is a Season 3 Mythic+ dungeon featuring 4 boss encounters with unique mechanics and challenging trash packs. 

**Key Dungeon Mechanic:**
- Destroy **5 Weapon Stockpiles** to summon Swampface (3rd boss)
- Located throughout the first area of the dungeon
